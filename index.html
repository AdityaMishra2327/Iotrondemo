About

Iotron, organized by the Signodes Club of IoT, is a premier event that embodies the innovative spirit of the Internet of Things (IoT). Designed to push the boundaries of technology, Iotron brings together passionate minds to explore, experiment, and create solutions that leverage the power of IoT. This event serves as a platform for participants to dive into the world of smart devices and connected systems, fostering creativity and collaboration to address real-world challenges.
